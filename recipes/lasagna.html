Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage: 1 lb
  • Ground Beef: 3/4 lb
  • Minced Onion: 1/2 cup
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, crushed
  • Crushed Tomatos: 1 can (28oz)
  • Tomato Paste: 2 (6oz) cans
  • Tomato Sauce: 2 (6.5oz) cans
  • Water: 1/2 cup
  • White Sugar: 2 tbsp
  • Dried Basil Leaves: 1.5
  • Fennel Seeds: 1/2 tsp
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chopped parsley: 4tbsp
  • Lasagna Noodles: 12
  • Ricotta Cheese: 16oz
  • 1 Egg
  • Mozzarella Cheese, sliced: 3/4 lb
  • Parmesan Cheese, grated: 3/4 cup

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatos, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley. Simmer, covered, for 1.5 hrs, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in water for 8-10 mins. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta with egg, parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C)
  4. Spread 1.5 cups meat sauce in bottom of 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles over sauce lengthwise. Spread with half the ricotta mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella slices. Spoon 1.5 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan. Cover with foil (to prevent sticking, spray with cooking spray or make sure foil doesn't touch cheese).
  5. Bake in heated oven for 25 mins. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 mins. Cool for 15 mins before serving.
